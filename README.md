# Flipkart-Customer-Segmentation-Targeted-Marketing-Intelligence
Data ingestion and multi-format parsing (CSV and JSON), data cleaning and normalization, relational schema design, SQL querying and RFM modelling, customer segmentation techniques, KPI design and metric derivation, BI dashboard development in Power BI, marketing strategy framing, business report writing, and campaign performance analysis.
Domain: E-Commerce Analytics | Customer Intelligence | Marketing Analytics | Retail Business Strategy
cleaned & integrated the raw dataset, built an RFM scoring model in SQL, assigned each of the 5,000 customers to one of ten behavioural segments, analysed their product interests and browsing patterns, and produced a marketing recommendation for each segment backed by numbers.
**Business Questions This Project Answers :**
● Who are our highest-value customers, and what do they buy?
● Which customers are showing early signs of churn before they are fully lost?
● How do purchasing patterns differ across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities?
● Which product categories drive the most revenue per customer segment?
● How does festive season behaviour differ from regular buying behaviour?
● Which customers respond best to discounts versus premium product launches?
● What is the measurable ROI difference between targeted and untargeted
campaigns?
● How should advertisement content and discount depth be personalised per
segment?

Customer Value Identification, Churn Prediction Using Behavioural Signals, Festive Season Revenue Concentration Analysis, Regional Demand and Tier-Based Personalisation, Browse-to-Purchase Gap Analysis, Campaign Channel Effectiveness by Segment, Discount Depth Optimisation, Customer Lifetime Value Modelling, Flipkart Plus Membership Conversion Opportunity, Cannot Lose Them Reactivation Modelling
